Description

Shoppers Drug Mart

The Future of Pharmacy

Client: Shoppers Drug Mart (Loblaw Digital)

Deliverables: Wireframes, Flows, Prototype

Role: UX/UI Design

Team: Disha Dhanwani, Phuong Nguyen, Dhruv Deva, Gareth Pereira

Design Opportunity: Improve the digital pharmacy experience to attract more patients.


 

Description

 

In our 2022 Human-Centred Design course at George Brown College, we collaborated with Loblaw Digital to revamp Shoppers Drug Mart's Digital Pharmacy feature. The goal was to address a decline in online transactions and patient retention. Our task: optimize the digital pharmacy experience for increased engagement and user satisfaction.

 

Concept Development

 

We began our research of the brand by examining its services and platform features. Subsequently, we delved into an analysis of competitors, identifying their strengths and weaknesses, and conducted precedent studies to uncover potential design opportunities. With a comprehensive understanding of the brand established, we proceeded to scrutinize the target audience and their online reviews to gain insights into their behavior.

Final Design

 

Following extensive research on the subject and recognizing the potential benefits of our features for users, I crafted high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes to enhance the overall navigational experience. Our design aimed to prioritize the functionality of the brand's numerous services. To maintain consistency with the brand guidelines outlined in the brief, I adhered to the Shoppers Drug Mart guidelines for the look and feel. Additionally, for UI components, I opted for a free UI kit by Antonin Bures, aligning seamlessly with our chosen visual language.
